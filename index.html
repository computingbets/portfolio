C://Booting...

In a creative pursuit, there is a difference between pausing and quitting. For stand-up comedy, I have recently hit pause a few times. Mainly, I'm in school for EEG Technician training and I don't live near the MBTA. Art is made by ordinary people. I am letting go of some storylines, expectations, allowing people and the biz, could be comedy business or even an art gallery, to be themselves. See the forest for the trees. +1yr without "trees". I hope I can continue to record my comedy and, unique to performing arts, get immediate feedback... which is also recorded.
