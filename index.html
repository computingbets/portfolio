Comedy Hiatus

D://Booting turmoil...

I, Mr. Stalewife Station Staleboat Must(y) Crus(ty) StalePale have stopped my stand-up comedy for 2023. enShort, I had/have pub-crawl-amateur-comedy burnout... Redundant? Perhaps. I held onto pain *un*like a World Heavyweight Title Belt. That IB... speaking ov, I need to change my view of the world and adapt for the truer & more real product. I haven't stopped challenging my fears, so the creation process remains. Remains in my terrible itch to take pain: from macro oppression, societal suppression, and my own emotional & psychic repression ( ...gression 🌪️🦣) and to turn it into momentary bliss, laughter. Drives me, still.
