C://Booting... H://Rallying...

In the creative pursuits, there is a difference pausing and quitting. For stand-up, I have paused a few times over the last 2 years. Mainly, I'm in training as an EEG Technician. I study full-time and have clinical work. I now live in Medford, which isn't close to public transit. Letting go of stressful storylines, expectations (for myself, others, and the scene), and allowing people and the arts biz to be themselves (monsters). See the forest for the trees and over 1yr without r/trees. I plan to continue to record my comedy and, what is unique in performing arts -> immediate feedback!
